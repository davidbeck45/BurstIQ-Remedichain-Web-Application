We believe surplus medication should be treated as a resource that can be managed and harvested to create medication access for vulnerable patients.

Who We Are

The RemediChain platform incentivizes the donation and disposal of surplus medication. Our ledger acts as a single source of truth for surplus medication across the globe, ensuring donated medications are connected with vulnerable patients, and expired medications are disposed of safely.

RemediChain gives vulnerable patients access to the medications they couldn't obtain any other way and protects the environment by ensuring surplus medication is disposed of properly.

How It Works

Step 1

Step 2

Login and upload the photo on the donation page

Step 2

Step 3

Step 4

Send us a photo from the drug disposal unit

Step 5
